About

"Welcome to Chicolati, the ultimate destination for chocolate lovers and milk tea fans alike! Each of our drinks is handcrafted with rich, quality chocolate, and perfectly balanced with the smoothness of milk tea, creating a unique treat that satisfies any craving. From classic chocolate milk teas to inventive, seasonal twists, every sip at Chicolati is a sweet moment of bliss. Join us, and experience the art of chocolate milk tea—crafted with love, served with joy."
